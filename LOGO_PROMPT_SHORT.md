# HolyGround Logo - Short Prompt

**Create a modern logo for "HolyGround" - a Bible social community platform.**

**Brand:** HolyGround - A Bible Social Community  
**Mission:** Social media platform for believers to connect, share Bible verses, ask questions, and grow in faith together.

**Style Requirements:**
- Modern, minimal, clean design (inspired by Reddit/X but with faith-based warmth)
- Warm, earthy color palette: deep brown (#5d4a2f), golden brown (#8b6f47), cream (#f5f1eb)
- Feel: Inviting, trustworthy, peaceful, approachable, contemporary
- Avoid: Cliché Christian symbols, overly ornate, traditional church imagery, dated looks

**Logo Concepts:**
1. Subtle "holy ground" elements (footprints, path, foundation) with spiritual connection
2. Community connection (network, circles, connection lines)
3. Growth/faith elements (plant, tree, roots) or spiritual ascent
4. Modern cross interpretation (geometric, minimal, integrated)
5. Subtle Bible/scripture reference (pages, open book) without being literal

**Technical:**
- Vector format (SVG)
- Works at sizes from 16px to large
- Versions: Full logo, icon only, wordmark only, monochrome
- Must work on light and dark backgrounds

**Symbolism:** Sacred space, community, growth, modern faith, accessibility, trust

**Deliverables:** Primary logo (horizontal/vertical), standalone icon, color variations, size variations, style guide.
